Laser Kart

Taking Go Karts to a whole new Level!

Laser Kart is Go Kart racing with PowerUp items, Speed Boosts, and Laser Blasters! As you race around the track, you pick up PowerUp items by driving directly over icons on the track. You’ll randomly power up with a Speed Boost or charge your Laser Blaster.

 

Speed Boosts give your engine an extra blast of power so you can fly by that slow poke in front of you!

 

When your laser blaster’s charged up, you can temporary knock out the engine of the racer in front of you! Pull up behind them, line up your shot, pull the trigger, and watch their warning lights and cockpit siren go off as their engine winds down to idle. You’ll zip past before they know what hit ’em!

Get Technical

Easy To Read Control Panel

LED lights and custom sounds make it easy to identify your PowerUp, or if you’ve been hit.

Working Break Light

All karts have a working break light and a “kill switch” than can be activated by a kart operator remotely.

Plug-in Configuration

Laser Kart does not require special Go Karts. Use your existing karts and make it more fun!
